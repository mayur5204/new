Voyager 1

Voyager 1 is a space probe launched by NASA on September 5, 1977. It is currently the farthest human-made object from Earth.

  • Launch Date: September 5, 1977
  • Distance from Earth: Over 14 billion miles
  • Primary Mission: Explore the outer planets
  • Current Status: Interstellar space
  • Notable Discoveries: Detailed images of Jupiter and Saturn

Voyager 2

Voyager 2 is a space probe launched by NASA on August 20, 1977. It is the only spacecraft to have visited all four outer planets: Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

  • Launch Date: August 20, 1977
  • Distance from Earth: Over 12 billion miles
  • Primary Mission: Explore the outer planets
  • Current Status: Interstellar space
  • Notable Discoveries: Detailed images of Uranus and Neptune
